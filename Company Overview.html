COMPANY OVERVIEW

About Us

Quicknet is one of the leading internet services in the KRG owned by a private company. Quicknet started operating, in 2022, in the city of Shaqlawa. Quicknet is, now, offering 4G LTE in some areas of KRG. Quicknet has established strategic partnership with some of the pioneers of internet services in the world.

Vision

Our aim is to have internet coverage in all of the KRG and bring Kurdistan the most advance internet services of the 21st century. Quicknet aims to serve the people of Kurdistan because we believe that a good internet service can keep people up to date and well connected to the rest of the world.

4G-Lte

LTE is a 4G wireless communication standard developed by the 3rd Generation Partnership Project (3GPP) that is designed to provide up to 10x the speed of 3G networks for mobile devices such as smartphones, tablets, netbooks, notebooks and wireless hotspots.
